## [1.0.0] - 2025-07-21
### Added
- Initial release of the JoinItem Skript
- Gives players a custom item in slot 8 on join, respawn, and world change
- Prevents dropping, moving, or losing the item
- Executes `/menu` when right- or left-clicked
